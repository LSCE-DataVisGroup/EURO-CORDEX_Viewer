Director of project: Robert Vautard
Robert Vautard has 25 years research experience in atmospheric and climate sciences, and has published over 100 peer-reviewed articles. He is responsible of the LABEX climate change research program and climate services development at Institut Pierre-Simon Laplace. He is also deputy director of the Paris Climate Consortium (www.gisclimat.fr), whose objective is to foster interdisciplinary research on climate change impacts.

Data and web engineering: Patrick Brockmann
Data scientist at CEA/DSM, member of the CalculS team at LSCE.
His research interests are data visualization, geospatial web applications, geoservices architectures and data processing in high performance computing environments.
In 2013, he was architect and project leader of the Global Carbon Atlas portal.
